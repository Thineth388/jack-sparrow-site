Who is Jack Sparrow?

Captain Jack Sparrow is the legendary pirate of the Seven Seas and the irreverent trickster of the Caribbean. A captain of equally dubious morality and sobriety, Jack is a master of self-promotion and self-interest. Despite his eccentricities, he possesses a keen wit and an uncanny ability to escape the most dire situations.

Born of the Sea

Jack was born on a pirate ship during a typhoon. He has dedicated his life to the sea, eventually becoming the Captain of the Black Pearl, the fastest ship in the Caribbean. His soul, however, is forever tied to the ocean, seeking freedom above all else.

Known Associates

  • Joshamee Gibbs: His loyal first mate and oldest friend.
  • Hector Barbossa: His rival, sometimes ally, and fellow Pirate Lord.
  • Will Turner: A blacksmith turned pirate, bound by destiny.
  • Elizabeth Swann: A governor's daughter who became a Pirate King.
