Legends of the Sea

Discover the most notorious pirates, cursed captains, and brave souls of the Caribbean.

Captain Jack Sparrow

Captain of the Black Pearl

A legendary pirate of the Seven Seas, and the irreverent trickster of the Caribbean. A master of self-promotion and self-interest.

"But you have heard of me."

Hector Barbossa

Pirate Lord of the Caspian Sea

A mutinous first mate turned cursed captain. Barbossa is a ruthless and cunning survivor who will do anything to maintain his freedom.

"The code is more what you'd call 'guidelines' than actual rules."

Will Turner

Captain of the Flying Dutchman

A blacksmith apprentice who discovers his pirate heritage. Driven by love and honor, he becomes one of the most formidable swordsmen.

"No matter what happens, I will free you."

Elizabeth Swann

Pirate King

A governor's daughter who traded her corsets for a cutlass. She evolves from a damsel in distress to the fierce Pirate King of the Brethren Court.

"This is madness!"

Davy Jones

Ruler of the Seven Seas

The monstrous captain of the Flying Dutchman. Tasked with ferrying souls to the afterlife, a broken heart turned him into a cruel tyrant.

"Do you fear death?"

Joshamee Gibbs

First Mate

Jack Sparrow's most loyal companion and right-hand man. A superstitious sailor with a deep knowledge of sea lore and legends.
